How to play/Violations

How to play

You must dribble the ball and get the ball in the basket in order to score. The team with the most points at the end wins.

There are five players on one team so ten playing in total. You can sub players in at certain points.

Common Violations

1.Double Dribble-Dribbling with two hands or dribbling again once you've stopped dribbling

2.Travelling-Taking more than two steps without dribbling.

3.Carying-Placing the hand under the ball while dribbling

4.Illegal screen-Moving while screening someone
